RECRUITMENT FOR NEW AKATSUKI MEMBERS.

Akatsuki, initially founded by Yahiko,Nagato and Konan in the wake of the Second Shinobi World War, began as a group dedicated to achieving peace through revolution. After Yahikos death, Nagato transformed Akatsuki into a more radical organization under the alias Pain, with a mission to collect the Tailed Beasts and impose peace through fear and control. The group, known for its distinctive red cloud cloaks and powerful rogue members like Itachi Uchiha and Orochimaru, operated with a complex hierarchy and diverse objectives. As the series progressed, Akatsuki's influence was challenged by Naruto Uzumaki and various allied forces. After the death of Hidan and Kakuzu, Pain alongside Itachi Uchiha and Konan have decided to recruit members inorder to continue with the tail beast collection.

Requirements Needed To Secure Your Spot

The following are requirements needed to join the AKATSUKI:

  • You need to be an S-ranked criminal. The rogue status is required.
  • Must put your heart and soul to the course.
  • Willingness to working in pairs.
  • Must put on the Akatsuki cloak all the time.
